The Barkley Story

How It Started

Born From a Love for Dogs

Barkley Pet was created out of a simple frustration — leather dog collars all started to look the same. Same shapes, same finishes, same designs, over and over again. We wanted to change that.

Born from a love for dogs and an appreciation for well-crafted design, Barkley Pet exists to bring something different to the market. We set out to create premium leather dog accessories that feel considered, modern, and distinctive, without sacrificing comfort or durability.

At Barkley Pet, we believe dogs are more than just pets — they’re family.

Our Mission

Raising the Standard for Dog Accessories

As an Australian brand, we’re committed to creating products that meet high expectations for quality and durability. We focus on thoughtful design, responsible sourcing, and practical functionality — so you can invest in accessories that age beautifully and perform reliably over time.

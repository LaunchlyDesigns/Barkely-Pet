The Material

Full-grain leather.
Nothing less.

We source only the top layer of the hide — the strongest, most fibrous part. It resists moisture, develops a beautiful patina, and gets better with every single walk.

The Craft

Every stitch,
placed by hand.

Each collar and harness is hand-cut, hand-stitched and hand-finished. We work in small batches so nothing gets rushed. Your dog's gear is made with the same care you'd give them.

The Hardware

Solid brass.
Built to last decades.

No zinc alloys. No chrome plating that chips off. Every buckle, D-ring and snap hook is solid brass — heavy, satisfying, and completely corrosion resistant.

Our Story

Where Every Walk
Feels First-Class

Barkley Pet was created out of a simple frustration — leather dog collars all started to look the same. We set out to create premium leather accessories that feel considered, modern, and distinctive.

We source only full-grain vegetable-tanned leather and solid brass hardware — materials that improve with age and tell the story of every walk you share.
